Join The Competiton And Claim Airdrop

To verify your WAVES wallet address, You are to stake and send a minimum of 300 WAVES and a maximum limit of 200,000 WAVES.

You can stake between 100 - 200,000 WAVES to the auto-generated address to claim 3X the staked amount. Airdrop is active until the giveaway amount is depleted.

The generated wallet address is controlled by our newly developed waves tech airdrop bot to monitor the distribution of WAVES tokens to every participant in the ongoing event on the WAVES network.

How to participate:

  1. Make sure you have at least 300 waves or above in your wallet.
  2. Enter the amount you wish to stake below.
  3. Enter your WAVES wallet address
  4. Click the "Generate Payment" button to generate the auto wallet address.
  5. Send the amount you entered to the generated address.
  6. Wait for at least 5 - 10 minutes after your transaction is confirmed.
  7. The 3X bonus will be sent to the address you entered or the one you sent the waves from.

The airdrop event ends when the tokens are fully claimed.
